Bengaluru Postbox

Welcome to the Bengaluru Postbox Community Blog!

Nestled in the heart of Southern India, Bengaluru, often referred to as the "Garden City" or the "Silicon Valley of India," is a vibrant metropolis where tradition meets modernity. Our city's rich history, diverse culture, and technological prowess create a unique backdrop for our thriving Postcrossing group.

About Our Group

Bengaluru Postbox is a close-knit group of postcard enthusiasts in Bengaluru mirrors the city's diversity, bringing together people from all walks of life with a shared passion for connecting across the world, one postcard at a time. Members include students, software and related fields' professionals, artists, retirees, and many others, each contributing a unique perspective to our gatherings. Through our collective love for postcards, we celebrate the art of letter writing and the joy of sending and receiving messages from around the globe.

Our Hobbies and Interests

Beyond our love for postcards, our members have a wide range of hobbies that reflect the dynamic spirit of Bengaluru. From avid travelers who share their journeys, our group is full of creativity and curiosity. Some of us are passionate about photography, capturing the essence of Bengaluru's bustling streets and serene parks, while others are enthusiastic in philately and numismatics, collecting pictorial and special cancellations issued in Bengaluru and other Indian cities.

A Glimpse into Our Lives

Our group is a blend of the city's fast-paced lifestyle and its deep-rooted traditions. Many of us work in Bengaluru's thriving tech sector, while others are involved in education or creative fields. Despite our busy schedules, we all find joy in the simple act of sending and receiving postcards, a welcome break from our daily routines. Our meetings are a space where we can unwind, share stories, and learn about different aspects through the postcards we exchange.

Join Us on Our Journey

Whether you're a longtime resident of Bengaluru or a visitor passing through, we invite you to join our Postcrossing journey. Through this blog, we aim to share our experiences, showcase our postcard collections, and celebrate the connections we've made across the globe. Stay tuned for updates on our latest meetups, member spotlights, and insights into the vibrant city of Bengaluru.
