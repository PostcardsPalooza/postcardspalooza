Postcrossing Meetup at Bengaluru GPO – A Humble Gathering

On November 9, 2024, the Bengaluru General Post Office (GPO) hosted a delightful Postcrossing meetup. A total of 10 enthusiastic participants came together to celebrate their shared love for postcards, marking yet another memorable event in Bengaluru’s Postcrossing journey.

Exchanging Ideas and Postcards

The meetup featured lively conversations, exchanging insights about postcards and philately. Attendees enjoyed signing postcards as a cherished tradition of Postcrossing meetups. The warm camaraderie among the participants made the gathering special despite its simplicity.

Custom-Designed Meetup Cards

To commemorate the occasion, two exclusive Postcrossing meetup cards were created. One featured the map outline of Karnataka state, while the other paid tribute to Kempegowda, the founding father of Bengaluru city. These cards were a unique keepsake for all attendees.

A Brief but Memorable Meetup

Although time constraints limited the scope of the gathering, the meetup succeeded in fostering connections and celebrating the spirit of Postcrossing. Participants departed with fond memories and beautifully signed postcards, looking forward to more such events in the future.

Special Thanks to Vijay Udupa

A heartfelt thanks to Vijay Udupa, who generously shared some of his collection with the attendees. His thoughtful gesture added an extra touch of joy and camaraderie to the meetup.
